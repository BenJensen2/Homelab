# Time
Understanding Time


[Greenwich Mean Time](https://greenwichmeantime.com) (GMT): Mean solar time at the Royal Observatory in Greenwich, London
- Reference for every time zone in the world.
- Same as Coordinated Universal Time (UTC)

Common Zones
- Central (CST - 6:00)
- Mountain (MST - 4:00)
- Eastern (EST - 5:00)
- Pacific (PCT - 3:00)
